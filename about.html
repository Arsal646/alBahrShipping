ABAF Shipping is a leading International Transportation Company which has been providing quality services to its clients since last many years. We provide same day, guaranteed shipments and have a 24/7 customer service line. We are fully licensed and registered with the UAE Ministry of Economy. As a reliable logistics provider company, we provide solutions to organizations engaged in international trade. We aim to be a one-stop solution for all shipping and logistics services through our diverse service offerings including Storage, Warehousing, Air Freight Services, Road Freight Services and Ocean Freight Services. To achieve the milestones, we have an experienced team with vast knowledge of the shipping industry who can support your needs in any way you can - whether you need to ship your goods across the city or halfway across the world. Contact with us today to discuss how we work in a brief.

ABAF Shipping is dedicated to providing excellent transportation services to our customers worldwide. We strive to maintain the highest standards of safety and customer satisfaction while ensuring that our prices remain competitive. We look forward to continuing to serve our customers with the same commitment and dedication in the years to come.

ABAF Shipping is committed to providing the best possible transportation experience for its customers by continuously innovating and improving its services. We aim to be the top international transportation company that provides quality services to its customers and helps them to achieve their business goals.
